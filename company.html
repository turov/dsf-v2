Company History

DSF is made up of a team of passionate employees working in offices around the globe. We have dual manufacturing, product development and distribution operations in North America, Central America and Caribbean Islands, South America, Asia, and Europe. For more than 10 years we have developed a portfolio of trusted brands, Cleanissimo, CleanKleen, and EcoKleen, around the world.

Working around the globe

With offices spanning across the globe, we have established a strong presence in key locations across Europe, Asia and Latin America. From bustling city centers to serene suburban areas, our offices are strategically positioned to serve our diverse clientele. Each office embodies our company's values and commitment to excellence, ensuring that we provide exceptional service and support to our customers no matter where they are. Our global network allows us to stay connected, collaborate seamlessly, and share knowledge and expertise across borders. We take pride in fostering a culture of inclusivity and diversity within our international offices, creating a vibrant and dynamic work environment that reflects the richness of different cultures and perspectives. Together, we work as a global team, united in our mission to deliver top-quality cleaning products and exceptional customer experiences to offices around the world.
