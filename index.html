About Us

Everyone at Instant Brands is focused on one purpose: to make togetherness easier. Every day. In every home. We’re home to an iconic family of brands – Corelle®, Corningware®, Instant Pot®, Pyrex®, Snapware®, Chicago Cutlery® and Visions® – who are already known for creating ingenious products to prepare and serve food. Soon we’ll be introducing ingenious solutions to help us all enjoy being together, everywhere in the home.

Creating a Healthy Haven

At DSF, we believe that a clean home contributes to a healthier and happier lifestyle. With our products, you can create a welcoming and hygienic space for yourself and your loved ones. We are dedicated to providing you with the tools you need to effortlessly maintain a clean and comfortable environment.

Clean with Confidence

Our motto is simple: Clean with Confidence. We want you to feel confident in the effectiveness and safety of our products, allowing you to focus on what truly matters – enjoying your home and spending quality time with those you care about.

Choosing the Best

Experience the difference of our cleaning products and let us help you transform your living rooms, kitchens, laundry rooms, and bathrooms into pristine spaces you'll love.
