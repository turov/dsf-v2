About Us

For more than 10 years, we have developed at DSF a portfolio of trusted brands with a range of purposeful products that caters to every corner of your home. We offer a wide range of products, all meticulously designed with the input of families like yours and incorporating cutting-edge technologies. Under our esteemed brands, Cleanissimo, CleanKleen, and EcoKleen, and our private label programs you gain more than just essential items. Our products represent a symbol of unparalleled comfort and a sustainable living.

Creating a Healthy Haven

At DSF, we are driven by our core belief, that a clean home fosters a healthier and happier life. With our innovative products, you can create a welcoming and hygienic space for yourself and your loved ones. We provide you with the necessary tools to effortlessly maintain a clean and comfortable environment.

Clean with Confidence

Our motto is simple: Clean with Confidence. We want you to feel confident in the effectiveness and safety of our products, allowing you to focus on what truly matters – enjoying your home and spending quality time with those you care the most.

Choosing the Best

Experience the difference of our cleaning products and let us help you transform your living room, kitchen, laundry room, bathroom, and other areas at home into pristine spaces you’ll love.
